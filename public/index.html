Frequently Asked Questions
How is the winner chosen? ＋
Winners are selected completely at random from all valid ticket holders once a pool sells out. The draw is performed by our system and every ticket has an equal chance of winning regardless of when it was purchased.
How do I know my entry was confirmed? ＋
As soon as your payment goes through you'll receive a confirmation email with your unique ticket ID(s). Keep this email as your proof of entry. If you don't receive it within a few minutes please check your spam folder.
Can I buy more than one ticket? ＋
Yes! You can purchase up to 10 tickets per transaction. Buying more tickets increases your chances of winning as each ticket is a separate entry into the draw.
When does the draw take place? ＋
The draw takes place once all tickets in a pool have been sold. There is no fixed date — it depends entirely on how quickly the pool fills up. You can track the progress on each pool card in real time.
How will I know if I've won? ＋
All participants are notified by email once the draw has taken place. If you win, you'll receive a winner email with details on how to claim your prize. If you don't win this time, you'll receive a thank you email with a link to enter future pools.
Is my payment secure? ＋
Absolutely. All payments are processed by Stripe, one of the world's most trusted payment providers. We never store your card details — everything is handled securely by Stripe's encrypted checkout.
Can I get a refund? ＋
Once a ticket has been purchased and entered into a pool it is non-refundable. This is because your ticket immediately forms part of the active draw. Please make sure you're happy with your entry before completing your purchase.
